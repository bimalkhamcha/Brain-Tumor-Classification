About Brain Tumor Classification

Project Overview

Welcome to the Brain Tumor Classification project, where we use Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify and diagnose brain tumors from medical images. The goal of this project is to assist in the early detection and classification of brain tumors, providing valuable insights for medical professionals.

How It Works

Our CNN model is trained on a dataset of brain images, learning to distinguish between different types of tumors. The model leverages the power of deep learning to extract meaningful features from the images, enabling accurate classification.
