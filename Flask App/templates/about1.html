Information about Brain Tumors

Glioma: Gliomas are a type of tumor that arises from glial cells in the brain or spine. They can be malignant and aggressive, affecting the brain's function.

Meningioma: Meningiomas are usually benign tumors that originate in the meninges, the layers of tissue covering the brain and spinal cord.

Pituitary Tumor: Pituitary tumors develop in the pituitary gland and can affect hormone production, leading to various symptoms.
